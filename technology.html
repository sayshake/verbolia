Intelligent SEO

Spellbinding tools to turbo boost your site ranking!

Verbolia is your answer to delivering optimized, highly ranked landing pages directly to your key audience.

Automated landing pages

Using a cutting-edge keyword generation engine, we analyze the keywords most likely to improve your site's SEO positioning. We analyze the potential and create dedicated pages for each keyword.

Semantic linking

We predict rankings of our pages with almost magical accuracy. On addition, our SEO landing pages are continuously monitored and adjusted to ensure optimal performance. New pages are added as needed to enhance SEO, whilst redundant pages are removed.

Automated content

Artificial Intelligence at your command.

Your website content in each of its forms is used to its full potential thanks to our NLP (Natural Language Processing) and NLG (Natural Language Generation) tools.

Write content with structured data

Your structured data is a treasure trove, used to generate intelligent content, personalized for your audience thanks to our AI-driven NLG tool. Based on your information, our tools can generate a whole host of variations.

Structuring unstructured data

Unstructured data is rich in information yet difficult to process without adequate resources or tools. We understand and analyze your unstructured content and data. Our NLP engines identify and extract the most relevant parts of your website and rewrite your content. In this way, you have 100 % original content with strong semantic context.

Analyze external content

Even the best of websites might lack some information. Not to worry, extra content such as open data, local news or government statistics are analyzed and used to generate content and increase the contextual relevance of a page. Pictures are also used as a source of information: recognition tools to generate content based on picture elements are used.
